## [CFMoto and Aspar Consider MotoGP Entry, 2027 Timeline Seen as Too Ambitious](https://www.the-race.com/motogp/cfmoto-aspar-motogp-plans-2027/) ⭐️ 3.0/10

CFMoto and the Aspar Team are evaluating a potential entry into MotoGP, but industry insiders consider 2027 an overly ambitious target for their participation. The partnership currently competes in the Moto2 and Moto3 classes before moving up to the premier category. A CFMoto-Aspar MotoGP entry would represent a rare new manufacturer entrant in the premier motorcycle racing series, potentially reshaping the competitive landscape. The 2027 timing is significant as it coincides with new MotoGP technical regulations, creating a potential window for new manufacturers to enter. The proposed entry would require CFMoto to develop 1000cc prototypes, representing a massive leap from their current 250-400cc Moto2 machinery. CFMoto currently manufactures motorcycles ranging from 125cc to 800cc, necessitating significant engineering investment for MotoGP's top specification bikes.
