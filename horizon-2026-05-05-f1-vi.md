## [F1 already working on downforce cuts for 2027](https://www.the-race.com/formula-1/f1-already-pursuing-downforce-reduction-plan-for-2027-cars/) ⭐️ 4.0/10

F1 teams will discuss ways to reduce downforce levels for 2027 in an attempt to improve cars' energy management. This represents an early-stage regulatory discussion as part of the upcoming chassis and power unit regulation review process. Reducing downforce could fundamentally change how F1 cars behave on track, potentially making them harder to drive but more efficient in terms of energy usage. This affects the core balance between aerodynamic performance and sustainability in Formula 1. The discussions focus on balancing downforce reduction with maintaining competitive lap times. Lower downforce affects tire degradation, cornering speeds, and the overall energy budget of hybrid power units. Teams are exploring how to achieve energy efficiency without sacrificing too much on-track performance.

rss · The Race F1 · May 5, 05:55

**Background**: Formula 1 has been in its hybrid power unit era since 2014, featuring sophisticated energy recovery systems including MGU-K (Motor Generator Unit-Kinetic) that recovers energy from braking, and MGU-H (Motor Generator Unit-Heat) that harvests energy from exhaust gases. The FIA has already finalized new 2026 power unit regulations incorporating 'green' initiatives to reduce F1's carbon footprint while maintaining high-performance hybrid technology.
