## [Vinales to miss yet another MotoGP round, Tech3 names stand-in](https://www.the-race.com/motogp/vinales-to-miss-yet-another-motogp-round-tech3-names-stand-in/) ⭐️ 3.0/10

Tech3 KTM rider Maverick Vinales will be forced to skip yet another round of MotoGP competition, withdrawing from the French Grand Prix at Le Mans. The team has named a replacement rider to fill in for the scheduled race. This marks another setback in what has been a challenging season for Vinales, who moved to Tech3 KTM this year after previous stints with Yamaha and Suzuki. The continued absences raise questions about his future in the sport and the team's competitive standing. Vinales has previously missed rounds this season, making this at least his second absence. The specific replacement rider named by Tech3 was not detailed in the available report.
