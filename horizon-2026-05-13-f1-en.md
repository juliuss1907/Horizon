## [Vasseur Transforms Ferrari's Risk-Averse Culture to Embrace Innovation](https://www.the-race.com/formula-1/ferrari-fred-vasseur-f1-design-ideas-innovation/) ⭐️ 3.0/10

Fred Vasseur has fundamentally shifted Ferrari's Formula 1 team culture since becoming team principal in January 2023, replacing Mattia Binotto. Under his leadership, the Scuderia has moved away from a risk-averse approach that previously 'didn't want to be exposed' toward embracing innovation and calculated risks. This cultural transformation is significant for Ferrari's competitive position in F1, where innovation and strategic risk-taking can determine race victories and championship outcomes. The shift reflects a broader principle in high-performance organizations: psychological safety enables experimentation and breakthrough thinking. Ferrari previously operated under a culture that prioritized avoiding mistakes over pursuing competitive advantages. This approach reportedly limited the team's willingness to pursue bold aerodynamic and design innovations that could differentiate their car from competitors.

Ferrari, as the oldest and most storied F1 team, carries unique pressure to perform, having last won a Drivers' Championship in 2007. Organizational culture in F1 directly impacts engineering decision-making, resource allocation, and ultimately competitive performance.

## [Formula E Gains Female Driver as Mercedes' Pin Joins Citroen](https://www.the-race.com/formula-e/formula-es-female-presence-grows-as-mercedes-pin-gets-citroen-role/) ⭐️ 3.0/10

F1 Academy champion Doriane Pin has been appointed as an official development driver for the Citroen Formula E team. She will test the Gen4 car this year, marking her entry into the world championship electric racing series. This appointment represents a growing presence of female drivers in top-tier motorsport categories. As Pin prepares to drive the technologically advanced Gen4 car with its record-breaking 1.8-second 0-60 acceleration, it could help pave the way for more women in electric racing. The Gen4 car features permanent active all-wheel drive and dual motors delivering 600kW (815bhp) of output. It also boasts enhanced 700kW regenerative braking and a 43% increase in race energy capacity up to 55kWh, providing significantly more energy for racing scenarios.

**Background**: F1 Academy is a female-only, Formula 4-level single-seater racing championship founded by the Formula One Group. It serves as a spec series where all teams compete with identical Tatuus F4-T421 chassis and Pirelli tires. The series aims to provide more accessible pathways for women in motorsport and break down entry barriers. Doriane Pin previously raced for Mercedes in F1 Academy before this move to Citroen.
