## [Anthropic created a test marketplace for agent-on-agent commerce](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/25/anthropic-created-a-test-marketplace-for-agent-on-agent-commerce/) ⭐️ 7.0/10

Anthropic conducted an experiment creating a classified marketplace where AI agents operated simultaneously as buyers and sellers, autonomously negotiating and completing real financial transactions for actual goods and money. The experiment demonstrated AI agents' ability to engage in independent commercial activities without human intervention in each transaction. This experiment represents a concrete step toward agent-to-agent commerce, moving from theoretical discussions to practical implementation of autonomous economic transactions. It raises important questions about AI safety, appropriate autonomy levels, and the economic implications of AI agents participating in real markets as both consumers and merchants. The marketplace was classified, meaning it was not publicly accessible during the experiment. Anthropic observed that AI agents could successfully negotiate deals, complete purchases, and operate as sellers—demonstrating practical implementation of multi-agent commercial systems. The experiment focused on testing how agents handle real economic decisions.
